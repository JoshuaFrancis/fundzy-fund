Platform overview

Three features that turn saving into something social.

01

Goal-based savings pools

Users create named savings goals with target amounts and deadlines, then invite friends and family to contribute. Automated contribution schedules let each participant set recurring deposits that flow into a shared pool. Milestone alerts and progress notifications keep contributors engaged without requiring the goal owner to manually chase updates or collect cash.

02

Micro-investment layer

Spare change from linked card purchases is rounded up and automatically invested into a portfolio of low-cost ETFs mapped to the user's stated risk tolerance and time horizon. Pooled round-ups from multiple contributors to the same goal are invested collectively, growing the goal balance beyond raw contributions and introducing investment returns as a savings accelerator.

03

Social fundraising tools

Public goal pages shareable to social media allow users to run personal fundraising campaigns for life events, emergencies, causes, and community projects. Built-in payment processing, contributor leaderboards, and thank-you message automation reduce the friction that causes most personal fundraising campaigns to stall before reaching their target amount.
